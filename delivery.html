Delivery & Ordering

Fresh, delivered to your door

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How to Order

Text 828.772.4255

Text us to place your order. We ask for at least 48 hours' notice so everything's made fresh. Prefer to pick up in person? Let us know when you text your order and we'll arrange a pickup time and location.

Delivery is $10, or free on orders over $200.

Meals arrive chilled with easy reheating instructions, delivered across Charleston, SC & the surrounding Lowcountry.
