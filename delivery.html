Before you order

A few helpful things to know before we get cooking.

Ordering Ahead

Please, 48 hours' notice

Place your order at least 48 hours ahead of when you'd like it delivered or ready for pickup, so every dish is made fresh.

Allergies

Just ask

Our dishes contain nuts and dairy. Have a food allergy or dietary need? Let us know when you order — we're happy to help where we can.

Payment

Venmo & Zelle

Payment is due at the time of order, via Venmo or Zelle.

Order Minimum

No strict minimum, yet

We don't have a strict order minimum at this time — just ask when you place your order. This page will be updated as our policies are finalized.
